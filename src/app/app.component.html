About H.O.P.E. Missions

H.O.P.E. Missions Ministries

Founded in 2004 H.O.P.E. (Housing Outreach Plus Evangelism) Missions Ministries has been serving the village of Mafredi in Belize, C.A. for 18 years.

Mafredi Village

Located in southern Belize, about 17 miles (28km) inland for the coastal city of Punta Gorda and about 18 miles (29kms) from the Guatemalan border. Mafredi is a small peaceful village nested against the edge of the Guatemalan mountains. It was here in 2000 that late H.O.P.E. Missions president Mitchell Osborne made a mission trip with the Methodist Church to help build the village church that ultimately lead to his love for the community and a home-away-from-home.
